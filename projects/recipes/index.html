Black Bean Chili

Chili is that cozy meal that you can always enjoy, no matter how much you eat. The beans and meat seem to melt into your mouth and with each bite, you feel even more at home.

This is an easy recipe that has always been a favorite in my household. This recipe means a lot to me and I hope it does to you too.

Texas Sheet Cake

Texas sheet cake offers a classic moist chocolate cake covered in a warm, fudgy frosting that melts right into the tender crumb. Baked in a large, simple sheet pan, it's the perfect crowd-pleasing dessert for any occasion that serves up pure chocolatey comfort.

This recipe is simple and is a perfect treat for a birthday, a party, or even if you just want some cake!
