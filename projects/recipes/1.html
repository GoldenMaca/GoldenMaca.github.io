Texas Sheet Cake

What does this mean to my family?

This is a recipe that has been in the family for a while and is a favorite of mine. I love how everything is so soft but also tastes so delicious. This recipe is great in a potato, on some fritos, or even by itself. I think this is a great recipe if you are feeding a lot of people because my family of four always has leftovers. I hope you enjoy this recipe as much as my family does!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup shortening
  • 1/4 cup cocoa
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Cooking Instructions

  1. In saucepan, combine water, butter, shortening, and cocoa; cook and stir until boiling occurs.
  2. In a bowl mix together flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon and salt.
  3. Add hot cocoa mixture to flour mixture and mix until smooth.
  4. Add buttermilk, eggs and vanilla and beat well.
  5. Pour into greased 151/2 x 101/2 x 1 inch pan.
  6. Bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes.

I reccomend chili with fritos, sour cream, avocados, and a drink.
